Wind Speed

Observed wind speed seems to hold a consistent range of less than 25 mph from -55 to 50 degrees latitude. Above ~50 degrees latitude we see higher wind speeds and likely below -55 degrees latitude we could expect the same but there is a lack of land below that latitude and so a lack of measurement stations.
